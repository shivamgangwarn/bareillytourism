Temple Highlights

Alakhnath Temple

A prominent temple of the Naga Sannyasis in Bareilly known for its spiritual ambiance and unique architecture.

Address:

State Highway 37, Phool Bagh, Quilla Chhawni, Civil Lines, Bareilly - 243001

Trivati Nath Temple

One of the most visited Shiva temples in Bareilly, symbolizing the city's cultural and religious essence.

Address:

66, Macnair Rd, Jawahar Nagar Colony, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh 243003

Dhopeshwar Nath Temple

A historic temple believed to be linked to the Pandavas, attracting many devotees and tourists alike.

Address:

8C8R+26F, End of, Dhopa Rd, Sadar Bazar, Cantonment, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh 243001
